Low Carb Chicken Curry

The Low Carb Chicken Curry - low carb curry recipe

The Low Carb Chicken Curry provides a nutritious, healthy chicken curry for everyone who loves asian cuisine, yet wants to look after their health. This delicios meal is served over rice and is best enjoyed hot. A great recipe for meal prepping!

Ingredients

  • 500g - Chicken breast
  • 400ml - Coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp - Soy sauce
  • 2 tsp - Curry paste red
  • 5 - Spring onions
  • 2 - Bell peppers
  • 1 glass - bamboo strips
  • 1 clove - Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces
  2. Cut the vegetables into small pieces
  3. Sear the curry paste in the wok
  4. Add chicken and sear as well
  5. Once the chicken has some colour, add vegetables and garlic
  6. After a minute or so add coconut milk and let simmer
  7. Add soy sauce and seasonings
  8. Put on plate with rice
  9. Enjoy!

Fitness Quark Bread

The Fitness Quark Bread - healthy bread alternative

The Fitness Quark Bread provides a healthier, protein-rich alternative to conventional bread. It is healthy yet delicious and it provides the body with many nutrients, while keeping the caloric density to a minimum. Indispensable for health conscious bread lovers!

Ingredients

  • 450g - Oatflour
  • 50g - Flaxseed meal
  • 1 Pack - baking powder
  • 1 tbsp - Salt
  • 500g - Low-fat quark
  • 3 - Eggs
  • 1 tsp - Sun flower oil
  • 60ml - Milk or Milk Substitute

Steps

  1. For a gluten free variant, oat flour and baking powder need to be gluten free
  2. Combine oat flour, flaxseed meal, baking powder and salt in a bowl
  3. Add low-fat quark, eggs, oil and milk
  4. Knead to a uniform dough
  5. Add to a baking form and bake at 180Â°C for 50 minutes
  6. Cut the top after about 15 Minutes of baking time
  7. Leave to cool for about 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
